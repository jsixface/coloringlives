Neduvasal School

Nutritious Free-Breakfast Scheme For 3 Months

One Headmaster’s attempt to modernize primary education for needy young kids in a govt school that was oft considered as a punishment school with poor connectivity, frequent students’, teachers’ absenteeism. His methodology of leveraging various government schemes, fundings from NGOs and trusts have proven instrumental in developing the infrastructure of the school and the nearby village, earning trust and respect of the locals. Rightly so, it has been bestowed “The Best school in the state “for holistic development by State Education Board on Aug 15,2010.
